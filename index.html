Who We Are

KASFAD International Ventures is a trusted building contractor and supplier of top-quality building materials. For years, we have been committed to providing reliable services, ensuring that our clients have the best materials to complete their projects with confidence and efficiency.

Our Mission

To be the go-to supplier for all construction needs, providing quality materials and excellent customer service to ensure successful projects for our clients.

Our Vision

To become the leading name in the building and construction industry across Nigeria and beyond, renowned for reliability, innovation, and unwavering commitment to excellence in every project we undertake.

Our Values

Reliability: Ensuring that our clients receive their materials on time, every time.

Quality: We only supply top-grade materials, certified to meet industry standards.

Customer Satisfaction: We strive to exceed expectations and build long-term relationships with our clients.
